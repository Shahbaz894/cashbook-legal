Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Welcome to CashBook. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using CashBook, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Identity Note: CashBook is developed by Shahbaz Zulfiqar. We are committed to financial data transparency and user privacy.

1. Data Collection & Cloud Synchronization

CashBook operates as a "Local-First" application. While data is stored in an encrypted Hive database on your device, we offer Optional Cloud Sync.

  • Authentication: We use Firebase Auth to manage account security (Email/Phone).
  • Cloud Storage: If enabled, your transaction data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore).
  • Security: We use Firebase App Check to verify app integrity.

2. Specific Permissions & Sensitive Data

To provide core features, the app requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Used exclusively for our "Voice-to-Text" feature. This allows you to speak your transaction details.
    Note: We do not store or share your voice recordings. Audio is processed ephemerally and then discarded.
  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS): Optional. Used solely to pick names for entries. Contact data stays on your device and is never uploaded.
  • Storage: Used to export PDF/CSV reports to your local gallery or files.
  • Internet: Required for account sync and Firebase services.

3. Data Security & Encryption

In compliance with modern safety standards, all data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS (SSL). Your financial records are private; we do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

4. Data Retention & Deletion Rights

You have the "Right to be Forgotten." You can:

  • Delete individual transactions or entire books within the app.
  • Request Account Deletion: Use the "Delete Account" button in Settings to permanently wipe all data from our Firebase servers.
