Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Welcome to CashBook. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using CashBook, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Identity Note: CashBook is developed by Shahbaz Zulfiqar. We are committed to financial data transparency and user privacy.

1. Data Collection & Cloud Synchronization

CashBook operates as a "Local-First" application. While data is stored in an encrypted Hive database on your device, we offer Optional Cloud Sync.

  • Authentication: We use Firebase Auth to manage account security (Email/Phone).
  • Cloud Storage: If enabled, your transaction data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore).
  • Security: We use Firebase App Check to verify app integrity and prevent unauthorized access.

2. Specific Permissions & Sensitive Data

To provide core features, the app requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Used exclusively for our "Voice-to-Text" feature. Audio is processed ephemerally to convert speech to text and is never recorded or stored on any server.
  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS): Optional. Used solely to pick names for entries. Contact data stays on your device and is never uploaded.
  • Storage: Used to export PDF/CSV reports to your local storage.
  • Internet: Required for account sync and Firebase services.

3. Data Security & Encryption

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS (SSL). We do not sell, rent, or share your financial data with advertisers or third parties.

4. Data Retention & Deletion Rights

You have the "Right to be Forgotten." In compliance with Google Play policies, we provide the following deletion options:

  • In-App Deletion: You can delete individual transactions or your entire account through the Settings menu within the app.
  • Online Request: If you cannot access the app, you may request account and data deletion by emailing shahbazzulfiqar894@gmail.com. We will process your request and wipe all associated Firebase data within 7 business days.
