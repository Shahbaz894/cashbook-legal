Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 09, 2026

Welcome to CashBook. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using CashBook, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Identity Note: CashBook is developed by Shahbaz Zulfiqar. We are committed to financial data transparency.

1. Data Collection & Cloud Synchronization

CashBook operates as a "Local-First" application. While data is stored in an encrypted Hive database on your device, we offer Optional Cloud Sync.

  • Authentication: We use Firebase Auth to manage account security (Email/Phone).
  • Cloud Storage: If enabled, your transaction data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore).
  • Third-Party Services: We use Google Firebase and Firebase App Check to verify app integrity and protect against fraud.

2. Specific Permissions

  • Contacts: Optional. Used solely to pick names for entries. Contact data is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Storage/Media: Used to export PDF/CSV reports to your device.
  • Internet: Required for account authentication and cloud backup services.

3. Data Security & Encryption

In compliance with modern safety standards, all data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS (SSL). We do not sell, rent, or share your financial data with advertisers or third parties.

4. Data Retention & Deletion Rights

You have the "Right to be Forgotten." You can:

  • Delete individual transactions or entire CashBooks within the app.
  • Request Account Deletion: Use the "Delete Account" button in Settings to permanently wipe all data from our Firebase servers. This action is irreversible.
