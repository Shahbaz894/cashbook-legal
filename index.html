Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Welcome to CashBook. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and financial data. This policy outlines how we handle information within our mobile application.

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

CashBook is designed as a "Local-First" application. All financial records, transactions, and payer names are stored locally on your device using an encrypted Hive database. We do not have access to your personal financial records unless you explicitly choose to use our encrypted cloud backup service.

2. Permissions

  • Contacts: Optional access to help you quickly select names for transactions. We never upload your contact list to our servers.
  • Storage: Required for exporting reports (PDF/CSV) and saving backups.

3. Data Security & Encryption

In compliance with Google Play requirements, all data transferred during cloud sync (if enabled) is encrypted in transit using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. Your data is never sold or shared with third-party advertisers.

4. Data Deletion Rights

Users have full control over their data. You may delete your account and all associated records directly from the App Settings. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.
